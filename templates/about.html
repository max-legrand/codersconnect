What we are

We are passionate coders bringing together fellow programmers and organizations

We provide oppurtunities to coders. Whether you are a college student looking for some real-world experience or an industry member looking to help the community, CodersConnect can help you find those volunteer projects you're looking for.

View Listings

01.Our Mission

Help non-profit organizations get the coding help they need through passionate volunteer coders.

02.Vission

To connect people all around the world with acts of kindness expressed through code.

03.Our Approach

We carefully curate jobs suitable for volunteer coders. We are here to help and will make sure no organization is taking advantage of your work. At the same time, we will also make sure to provide organizations with trusted volunteers.
